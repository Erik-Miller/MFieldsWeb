Our Mission

Three generations and over 75 years later we are still providing our customers with quality guaranteed meats and friendly personal service. By maintaining our strong values, we continue to grow as a company as well as contribute to our community.

Who We Are

Miesfeld’s Meat Market (since 1941) is a family-owned and operated meat market located in Sheboygan, Wisconsin. Our knowledgeable, dedicated staff is committed to providing our customers and partners with quality products and services that exceed their expectations.
